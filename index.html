About Me

Hi! I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the CNRS, working in the Institute Genetics & Development of Rennes. I recently completed my PhD at the University of Technology of Compiègne, where my research focused on Machine Learning and Deep Learning, exploring their applications in Health/Biomedical fields, with a particular interest in Biomedical Signal and Image Processing. Before joining UTC, I worked as a Data Analyst at the Centre Oscar Lambret in the Clinical Research and Innovation team.

I hold a MSc in Data Science, a BSc in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Lille, and a BSc in applied mathematics from the University of Nouakchott.

Comprehensive Review of Feature Extraction Techniques for sEMG Signal Classification: From Handcrafted Features to Deep Learning Approaches
This survey aims to provide a comprehensive overview of feature extraction techniques for sEMG signal classification ranging from the handcrafted to the learned features.
Sidi Mohamed Sid'El Moctar, Imad Rida, Sofiane Boudaoud

A Comprehensive Review of sEMG-IMU Sensor Fusion for Upper Limb Movements Pattern Recognition
This review provides a comprehensive analysis of sEMG-IMU sensor fusion techniques for upper limb movement pattern recognition. It offers detailed insights into the signal generation mechanisms of both surface electromyography (sEMG) and inertial measurement units (IMU), and critically explores multisensory fusion strategies aimed at enhancing recognition accuracy and reliability.
Honglei Zhang, Sidi Mohamed Sid'El Moctar, Sofiane Boudaoud, Imad Rida
Information Fusion
Paper
iEMG-Based Diagnosis of ALS and Myopathy using 1D-CNN
In this study, we present a lightweight, high-performance 1D-CNN approach for multiclass classification of EMG signals—capable of accurately distinguishing ALS, myopathy, and healthy subjects with minimal preprocessing.
Sidi Mohamed Sid'El Moctar, Chawki Nasrallah, Imad Rida, Sofiane Boudaoud
